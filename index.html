About Us

Electrical Innovations, LLC is an electrical company based out of Timberlake, Ohio. For the past 10 years Electrical Innovations has been providing services all across Northeast Ohio. We are a small, family-owned and operated company that values honesty and integrity in all aspects of our business.

Electrical Innovations was founded by Mike Kovalkevich in 2014. Mike started his 5 year apprenticeship with Local 38. After completing his apprenticeship working with a few different companies along the way, he became the go to service guy. Mike found that he loved service work because he loved the challenges it would sometimes bring and allowed him to get creative to get the job done. After 13 years working for other electrical companies Mike decided to start his own company. Mike has an uncompromising commitment to excellence and quality work. He is dedicated to making sure his customers are satisfied with the work his company does for them.

Mike and his dedicated team provide innovative ideas and suggestions tailored to your projects. Our services are customized to your individual needs, paying close attention to detail on each project we are a part of. You can be certain, when you hire us that we go above and beyond to treat you and your home with the utmost respect.
